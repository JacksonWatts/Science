this web page will be on cheetahs (look how cute it is!)

cheetahs live mainly in the African savanna and are know for being the fastest land animal.


the fastest land animal is in danger of extinction even though this may sound frightening there are a few things we can do,

  1. the first thing we can do is to try and stop the illegal pet trade.
  2. eve nthough stopping the illegal pet trade may be a intimidating task another thing we could do is to stop expanding into cheetah teritory.
  3. one last thing we could do would be to stop poaching
if cheetahs do go extinct it will have great effect on many other animals in their ecosystem so it is vital that we fix this problem before it is too late.
